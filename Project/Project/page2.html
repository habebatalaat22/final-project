Faculty of Computers and Data science Overview :

The Faculty of Computers and Data Science, particularly the one at Alexandria University, is a relatively new but rapidly growing institution focused on
computer science and data-related fields. It offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in areas like Computer Science, Business Analytics, Artificial Intelligence ,Cybersecurity and Media Analytics. The faculty aims to produce graduates equipped with the skills to tackle the challenges of the data-driven world, contributing to technological advancements in various sectors.

Data Science

Data science is a multidisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It combines aspects of mathematics, statistics, computer science, and domain expertise to analyze data, uncover patterns, build predictive models, and ultimately guide decision-making.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access or criminal use. It involves implementing various measures to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information. In essence, it's about safeguarding digital assets from cyber threats like malware, ransomware, and phishing attacks.

Business Analytics

Business analytics is the practice of using data, statistical analysis, and business intelligence to gain insights and make informed decisions to improve business performance. It involves collecting, cleaning, analyzing, and interpreting data to identify trends, predict outcomes, and ultimately, drive business growth. Essentially, it's about turning raw data into actionable insights that can be used to solve problems, optimize processes, and identify new opportunities.

AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, problem-solving, decision-making, and even creativity. AI systems achieve this by processing data, recognizing patterns, and adapting their behavior to achieve specific goals.

Media Analytics

Media analytics is the process of collecting and analyzing data from media sources to gain insights into communication, audience behavior, and brand performance, with a strong focus on earned media (third-party coverage) and shared media (social conversations). It helps understand public sentiment, measure the effectiveness of public relations and communication campaigns, and make data-driven decisions to inform strategy and optimize content across various media platforms, including news, social media, blogs, and podcasts.
